## Learned
- Send arguments into a generator in [Python]({{< ref "anotacoes/Python" >}})
	```python
	In [1]: def generator():
	    ...:     while True:
	    ...:         received = yield 'DATA'
	    ...:         print('Received:', received)
	    ...:
	
	In [2]: g = generator()
	
	In [3]: next(g)
	Out[3]: 'DATA'
	
	In [4]: g.send(1)
	Received: 1
	Out[4]: 'DATA'
	
	In [5]: g.send(2)
	Received: 2
	Out[5]: 'DATA'
	```
