- `jq`
    * sort output by key: `jq -S`
    * cast strings->JSONs `jq '.<key> |= fromjson'`
    * cast multiple strings->JSONs `jq '.<key> |= fromjson | .<another-key> |= fromjson'`
    * cast string->JSON in a list `jq 'map(.<key> |= fromjson)'`
