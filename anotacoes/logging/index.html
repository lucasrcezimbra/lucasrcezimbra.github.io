Plaintext vs Structured Link to heading

  • Plaintext
    • pros: most commom; human-readable;
    • cons: hard to parse;
  • Structured
    • pros: easy to parse; easy to search/filter/aggregate;
    • cons: verbose; less human-readable (can be solved by showing only the message);
